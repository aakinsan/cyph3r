Wireless Key Ceremony

This module will guide you through the process of securely generating, storing and distributing cryptographic keys. To mitigate the risk of exposure before distribution, these keys will be encrypted with the PGP public key of the recepient.


This app only accepts PGP public keys that are less than 5KB and are in the "ASCII Armored format" i.e. public keys with the begin and end headers of -----BEGIN PGP PUBLIC KEY BLOCK----- and -----END PGP PUBLIC KEY BLOCK----- respectively.


While strong measures have been taken to secure the key generation and distribution process, it is important that you follow best practices for securing the keys. At a minimum, ensure that the devices receiving the keys are trusted and that no unauthorized individuals have access to your environment.
